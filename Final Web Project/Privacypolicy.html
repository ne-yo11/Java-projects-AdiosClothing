Privacy Policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information by Adios Clothing, the operator of adios clothing. We are committed to protecting the privacy of your personal information and to being transparent about the types of information we collect, how it is used, and with whom it is shared.

Information Collection

We collect personal information from you when you visit our Website, place an order, create an account, sign up for our newsletter, or participate in a survey or promotion. The types of information we may collect include your name, email address, postal address, telephone number, and payment information. We may also collect information about your browsing and purchasing history.

Use of Information

The information we collect is used to provide you with a better shopping experience, to process and fulfill your orders, to communicate with you, and to improve our Website and services. We may use your information to send you promotional materials, to notify you of new products or sales, and to personalize your experience on our Website.

Sharing of Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except as necessary to fulfill your order, such as to process your payment or to ship your order. We may also share your information with service providers who assist us in operating our Website and conducting our business, such as hosting companies and payment processors. We will also disclose your personal information as required by law or in response to a valid court order.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no data transmission over the internet or any wireless network can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time. You can also opt-out of receiving promotional materials from us. To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email or postal address provided below.

Changes to this Policy

We may make changes to this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will post the new policy on our Website and update the "effective date" at the top of the policy. We encourage you to review our Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting the personal information we collect.
